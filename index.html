Dashboard

Quickly see your pet's feeding schedule at a glance. Track when they were last fed and when their next meal is due. Never miss a feeding time again with our smart notification system.

Organize pets by personal and family groups to keep everything neatly categorized.

Manage Stock

Keep track of your pet food inventory in real-time. See how many boxes or packets you have left and get estimates on how long your current stock will last.

Receive smart insights about which pets need more food and when you should restock.

Smart Insights

Get detailed insights into your pets' health and feeding patterns. See missing daily calories and recommended extra portions to ensure your pets are getting the nutrition they need.

Our smart analytics help you make informed decisions about your pets' care.

Track Health

Monitor important health metrics to keep your pets healthy. Track calorie intake, weight, and other vital information to ensure your pet is getting the proper nutrition.

Get personalized recommendations based on your pet's breed, age, and weight.

Additional Features

Families

Share your pets' feeds, health information, and more with family members - all synced in real-time. Create or join family groups to collaborate on pet care.

Feed History

Track all feeding events with detailed information including time, portion size, and food type. See your pet's feeding history organized by day.

Pet Profiles

Easily view and manage each pet individually. Create detailed profiles with important information about your pets.
